# E-commerce-Revenue-Retention-Analysis
Analyzed 50,000+ e-commerce transactions to identify high-value 'Power Users' and calculate category revenue share to optimize inventory velocity.
Business Questions Addressed:

    What percentage of revenue does each category contribute?

    Who are our most loyal customers (Power Users)?

    Which categories are moving the fastest?

Technical Skills Demonstrated:

    Window Functions: Used SUM() OVER() for revenue share calculations.

    Data Aggregation: GROUP BY and HAVING for customer segmentation.

    PostgreSQL Syntax: Handled integer division and string concatenation (||).

Key Insights:

    Example: "The 'Electronics' category accounts for 40% of revenue but only 10% of order volume, suggesting a high-margin, low-velocity model."
